Process Work 4: Annotate a Secondary Source

Prompt: "Create at least one secondary source annotation for a scholarly, peer-reviewed article you have found in the UCF library database. The annotation should... include an MLA or APA citation for the article, followed by the annotation. Your annotation should be organized into two paragraphs. ...include the following:

  • First paragraph, summarizing the main arguments/claims, define key terms, and summarize the author's conclusions. Focus mainly on the aspects of the article that are relevant to your research.
  • Second paragraph, analyzing will be an explanation of how your secondary sources relate to each other and/or how this source can be used in your own project."
(Rachel Marks, "Process Work 4: Annotate a Secondary Source")
