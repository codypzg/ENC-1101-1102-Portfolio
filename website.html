A Passage about Writing

To say Composition I was immensely rewarding in how it enabled me to tap into a subject that I enjoy so much would be saying little. Prior to it, my proudest piece of writing had been my college admissions essay, as you will hear about in later portfolio pieces. But as I come out the other end, I feel equally proud of each Major Assignment.

I have to give credit to Professor Marks for structuring the course in such a simple, yet excellently effective manner. Every assignment worked up to this website in a way that I only know now. I have deeply appreciated the chance to diversify, and incorporate, my learning from the technology classes of my degree to this one.

My abilities to write and craft stories have expanded their horizons so greatly that I barely even know how to articulate it. I hope this Portfolio, and the comparisons that can be made between its contents, may speak to such growth in itself.
